Bad Bunny

The World's #1 Artist

Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio

Puerto Rican rapper, singer, and record producer.

"The King of Latin Trap"

Bad Bunny was born in Bayamón, Puerto Rico, and loves his native country.

"Debí Tirar Más Fotos" is a love letter to Puerto Rico

Bad Bunny has three Grammy Awards, seventeen Latin Grammy Awards, eight Billboard Music Awards, and thirteen Lo Nuestro Awards.

He was the most streamed artist on Spotify in 2020, 2021, 2022, and 2025.

Bad Bunny has released eight albums in the last eight years

These albums feature collaborations with renowned artists such as J Balvin, Drake, Rosalía, and more.
